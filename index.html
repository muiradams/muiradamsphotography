Meet the Photographer

I’ve been a full-time wedding photographer for the past 8 years, and I love working with couples who are madly in love, and want their photos to show it! As you’ll see from my photography, I specialize in beautiful outdoor weddings, and pay special attention to finding the most romantic backdrops and amazing light. My goal is to help couples feel comfortable enough to be themselves as I capture authentic moments.

Okay, a little more about me, and then I want to hear more about you!… I love love, and I LOVE photos that make me feel something! I love my wife. She is my best friend, and my perfect fit. Our wedding day was the best day of my life, and marrying her was the best decision I’ve ever made. We had a Nepali ceremony on Saturday, celebrating my wife’s cultural heritage, and a Christian ceremony on Sunday that incorporated our mutual faith. Needless to say, we’re big fans of marriage! When it comes to raising our son, Blake, we are a team, and I wouldn’t want it any other way. I try to make serving others a big part of my life. I love to cook and try new foods. I am an adventurer at heart. I travel frequently to feed my hunger for culture, adventure, and good food! Italy, Ethiopia, Spain, Malta, India, and Japan are among my favorite places I’ve visited.

I’d love to get to know you and learn more about your story! I’m sure we’ll find that we have a lot in common!

Photography Classes

"Auto to Advanced: Learning to use your DSLR like a Pro"

This is a four week photography class for anyone with a digital SLR who wants to learn how to move beyond their camera’s “auto” mode and create photos like a pro. In the last 5 years since I started teaching, I've had over 175 students attend this class. Though it starts with basic concepts, I move deep into each topic and share everything I consider when creating a professional quality photograph. Because of this, my classes are great for anyone with a digital SLR, whether you’re a beginning photographer, an advanced amateur, or anywhere in between.

What we'll cover:
  • Week 1: "Understanding Exposure: Aperture, Shutter Speed, & ISO."
  • Week 2: "Understanding Your Camera: Histograms and Metering."
  • Week 3: "Controlling the Image: Lighting and Composition."
  • Week 4: "Creating the Image: From Focus to Photoshop."
